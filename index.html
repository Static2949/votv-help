My Kerfur/Kerfur-O keeps getting stuck in the river!

Go to Keybinds (in settings), scroll down and set a hotkey for snow/winter mode.
That will turn river into ice, allowing him, you, and even your ATV to simply slide across it.
Whenever you send out Kerfur to do things, you should enable winter mode - he will cross the ice instead of getting stuck and drowning.

Nails don't work!

Hold a nail (in hands), put it where you want things to connect - it would "freeze" in that position, then hit it with a hammer (or just buy the nailgun).
If your nail falls off, or doesn't connect stuff, then there are two potential issues you may have:
1. Try aiming it somewhere else (even a few pixels to the side could be sufficient), or change angle (again, just a bit of rotation could be sufficient).
2. Objects have two "bodies" - one is a model mesh, basically, what you can visually see. Other is a collider - something that is invisible and is used for collisions (player with object for example). Nails connect objects by their invisible colliders, but not visible mesh!
Basically, imagine a cube, which would be mesh and which you can see, and a sphere inside it, which would be invisible collider. So, if you would want to attach the cube to something - you would not be able to attach it by putting nails onto its corners.

Running low on scrap!

Shoveling trashpiles (these piles that connect to ground whenever they touch ground) - would provide you with pile-specific resources.
You can also dig various items from the ground, but you would need a metal detector to find these spots.
Sometimes you get trash from which you can craft scrap, or you could try disassembling an item.
You could also try buying items just to disassemble them.
